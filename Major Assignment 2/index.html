About Me

If I had to describe scuba diving in one word, it would be freedom. From the moment I entered the water, all of my worries and stress disappeared. The only thing that was on my mind was how peaceful and relaxing the ocean was. All I could hear was my breathing… breathing in.. breathing out.. while enjoying this new world and its inhabitants around me. Life underwater, as I discovered during my scuba diving experience, was more beautiful than I imagined, and the tranquillity was unparalleled to anything I have experienced before. Something I hadn’t really considered was just how human-centric our lives are on land. Everything seems to revolve around ourselves, with our own needs and desires. However, when you enter the marine world as a diver, it’s a different story entirely. Every dive is a unique experience and I enjoy it without thinking that my life is not perfect or that I could have done more. Scuba diving erases all these from my mind and offers me the wonderful feeling of freedom.
